Instant coffee
2022

Realizing that powdered coffee takes a long time to prepare, manufacturers have begun to think of ways to help users enjoy coffee more conveniently. During the production process, they have premixed in the coffee powder with flavorings and additives to achieve a desired taste. Users just need to put this powder into boiling water, mix it up and enjoy, it only takes 30 seconds to make a cup of coffee. Instant Coffee was born to serve an increasingly busy society. The advantage of instant coffee is that it is quick, the taste has been prepared and calculated with a moderate amount, so you can always enjoy the best cup of coffee. However, it has a drawback. It is quite large that the flavors mixed inside will lose some of the pure coffee taste.

Pure roasted coffee
2022

This type of coffee is roasted and ground into a powder. This is a coffee made from 100% natural coffee beans, without the shadow of any other additives such as beans, corn or grains. The advantage of pure coffee is to keep what is most characteristic of coffee, connoisseurs and lovers of this bean will love to use pure roasted coffee.
